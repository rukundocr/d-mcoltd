Using this smart blind stick, a visually impaired person can walk without anyone's help. The smart blind stick automatically detects the obstacle in front of the person and give him a response to the person by vibrating the stick and also with a warning sound..

Visually impaired people are the people who can't identify smallest detail with healthy eyes. Those who have the visual acuity of 6/60 or the horizontal extent of the visual field with both eyes open less than or equal to 20 degrees, these people are considered blind. Such people are in need of aiding devices for blindness related disabilities. 10% of blind have no usable eyesight at all to help them move around independently and safely. The electronic aiding devices are designed to solve such issue
